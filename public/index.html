What Will Happen

In the event that FreeRossDAO wins the auction, the NFT will be fractionalized into $ROSS and distributed pro-rata, weighted by contribution. $ROSS will serve as governance rights within FreeRossDAO and fractional ownership of the “GENESIS COLLECTION” NFT.

In the specific event that FreeRossDAO wins the auction with a bid less than the contributed ETH, the leftover funds will be held in the treasury, governed by FreeRossDAO and available for future impact.

In the event that FreeRossDAO loses the auction, contributions will be available for full redemption.

Free Ross DAO Mission

Following a successful bid, the DAO will be lead by Trippy, Chief Freeing Officer and collectively use leftover funds raised too continue to pursue efforts to get Ross freed and further the awareness for prison reform.

Governance

Once the bid to acquire the Genesis Collection is successful, contributors will be given $ROSS tokens in return for their contributions pro-rata, serving as the governance token in Free Ross DAO. Holders of $ROSS will be able to vote on proposals. If the bid is not successful, the DAO will be dissolved with participants able to redeem their contribution in full.

Proposals will be created by Trippy and a future DAO council, with the multssigners below acting as the executioners of approved proposals.
